Our Process

Clusta.ai re-humanises the conventional career path by inverting the traditional recruitment model and putting you at the heart of the process.

Apply

We take the time to look over ever application we get. We accept applications all year round and you can apply in under 10 minutes.

Chat

If your application was successful, you will get a call from one of our career adviosrs to learn all about you. What your goals and ambitions are.

Coaching

We then spend up to 3 months working with you get you ready for the best job possible. Everything from how to handle the interviews to developer skills missing from your CV

Job Offer

Once we feel you're ready, we put you in front of some of the biggest companoes, offering some of the best roles out there. Each offer will be tailored to you.

Our Ethos



We believe that the key to an organisation's success lies with having extraordinary talent. This makes you worth more than you know and we know this. That’s why we put you at the heart of everything we do. We learn all about you, find out what you’re passionate about and then find you the right opportunity for you. We don’t look to fill an organisation’s vacancy - we engage in proactive career placement and create that opportunity for you in a company that culturally fit’s you.
